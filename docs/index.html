Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Surprise Ride respects your privacy. This application is designed to help users discover interesting places and create sightseeing routes.

Location Data

The app may access your device location only to determine a starting point for route generation. Location information is processed locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

Visited Places

The application stores selected and visited places locally on your device to improve future route suggestions. This information is not shared with third parties.

Third-Party Services

The app uses public map and geolocation services such as OpenStreetMap and related APIs to display maps and discover places. These services may process requests according to their own privacy policies.

Data Collection

Surprise Ride does not collect personal information such as name, email address, phone number, or payment information.
