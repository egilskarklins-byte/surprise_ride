Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Surprise Ride ("the App") helps users discover interesting places nearby and plan scenic routes.

Information We Collect

The App may use:

  • Your location (only after permission is granted).
  • Map data from Google Maps and OpenStreetMap.
  • Publicly available place information.

The App does not collect personal information such as your name, email address or phone number.

Location

Location is used only to generate routes around your current position. Location is never sold or shared for advertising purposes.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Maps
  • OpenStreetMap

These services may process technical information according to their own privacy policies.

Data Storage

Visited places and application settings may be stored locally on your device.

Advertising

The current version of the App does not display advertisements. Future versions may introduce optional advertising or premium features.
